Welcome to Inventory Control System

Our system helps you manage your inventory efficiently. With easy-to-use interfaces and powerful features, you can streamline your product management process and stay organized.

Key Features

  • Manage Products: Add, update, view, and delete product details.
  • Manage Product Categories: Add, update, view, and delete categories.
  • Intuitive Interface: User-friendly interface for smooth navigation.
  • Responsive Design: Accessible on all devices, including desktops, tablets, and mobile phones.

Get Started

To start managing your inventory, navigate to the Products or Product Categories page using the navigation menu above.
